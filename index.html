About Us

Metal Mayhem was formed in 2010 when five friends from São Paulo, Brazil decided to combine their passion for heavy metal with progressive elements and Brazilian rhythms.

After their groundbreaking debut album "Chaos Theory" in 2012, the band quickly gained international recognition for their technical prowess and energetic live performances.

Today, Metal Mayhem continues to push musical boundaries, blending traditional metal with experimental sounds that challenge the genre's conventions.

With three platinum albums and worldwide tours, the band remains committed to creating music that resonates with fans while exploring new sonic territories.
